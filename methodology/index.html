Methodology

The public methodology, transparency ledger, and conceptual basis of the AHFPF.

Conceptual Basis

The framework is built on an adaptive systems approach. It is non-ideological and assumes that impact is the result of structural alignment rather than just good intentions.

Why P5 is the Missing Pillar

Traditional models stop at Impact (P4). AHFPF identifies Communication (P5) as the infrastructural layer that dictates whether P1-P4 function as a coherent system. The concept of Communication Debt explains how initiatives fail despite strong execution.

Transparency Ledger

This repository serves as a Transparency Ledger. Every change to the methodology is tracked publicly via GitHub to ensure archival legitimacy and open methodology culture.
