Pillar 2

Execution

The implementing body (partner, agency, team, volunteers).

Definition

Execution encompasses the operational mechanisms translating vision to action. It's the practical, on-the-ground reality of getting things done.

P5 Connection (Communication)

Communication is required for Coordination. Without P5, the executing body is disconnected from the Entity's vision (Implementation gap).

"AHFPF proposes that societal impact is not determined solely by initiative quality, but by whether value successfully travels between Entity, Execution, Initiative, and Impact through Communication as infrastructure."
