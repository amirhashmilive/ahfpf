Pillar 4

Impact

Measurable change in quality of life, well-being, or capability.

Definition

Impact is the ultimate outcome. It is the real-world change that justifies the existence of P1, P2, and P3.

Observable vs Invisible Impact

Impact may exist objectively but remain socially invisible when communication systems fail.

P5 Connection (Communication)

Communication enables Perception and Feedback. Without P5, Impact is unperceived, and the Entity never receives the feedback necessary for adaptation.

"AHFPF proposes that societal impact is not determined solely by initiative quality, but by whether value successfully travels between Entity, Execution, Initiative, and Impact through Communication as infrastructure."
