Pillar 1

Entity

The initiating actor (individual, organization, company, government).

Definition

The Entity is the origin point of any initiative. It possesses the resources, the mandate, and the vision to enact change. Without a clearly defined Entity, there is no accountability.

Key Questions

  • Who is initiating the action?
  • What is their core mandate and motivation?
  • Do they possess the necessary resources and legitimacy?

P5 Connection (Communication)

Communication determines how the Entity is perceived. Without P5, the Entity remains unknown to the public or the beneficiaries, leading to trust deficits and a lack of legitimacy.

"AHFPF proposes that societal impact is not determined solely by initiative quality, but by whether value successfully travels between Entity, Execution, Initiative, and Impact through Communication as infrastructure."
