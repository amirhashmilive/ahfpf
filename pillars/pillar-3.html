Pillar 3

Initiative

The action, program, project, or intervention.

Definition

The Initiative is the concrete activity being performed—the service, the product, the policy, or the campaign.

P5 Connection (Communication)

Communication determines Accessibility. Without P5, the Initiative remains unaccessed or misunderstood by the target audience.

"AHFPF proposes that societal impact is not determined solely by initiative quality, but by whether value successfully travels between Entity, Execution, Initiative, and Impact through Communication as infrastructure."
